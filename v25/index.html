Music

Kind of Blue

Miles Davis · 1959

Murakami's prose moves like modal jazz — unhurried, atmospheric, full of space. Miles Davis understood that what you leave out matters as much as what you play.

Dining

Ichiran Ramen

Hakata, Japan

The solo dining booths and ritual simplicity mirror the solitary, contemplative worlds Murakami builds. Every bowl is a scene in one of his novels.

Cinema

In the Mood for Love

Wong Kar-wai · 2000

Longing, restraint, and rain-soaked streets. Wong Kar-wai films the same emotional register Murakami writes — things felt but never quite said.
